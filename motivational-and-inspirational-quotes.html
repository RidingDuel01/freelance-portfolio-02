Awesome Motivational and Inspirational Quotes

Hey guys! Enjoy these motivational & inspirational quotes. And while you are reading these quotes, I want you to really think about how these quotes if at all, have an impact in your life. Did these quotes help you realize something from a different angle? Did they helped you get a better understanding of something you didn't understand before? Did they get you to go through with an idea that you planned on throwing away? Don't just read these quotes without giving it a good run on the old treadmill, lol. Happy thinking!

“Success is nothing more than a few simple disciplines, practiced every day.” -- Jim Rohn

"What you get by achieving your goals is not as important as what you become by achieving your goals." -- Zig Ziglar

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor. -- Elon Musk

"Don't watch the clock; do what it does. Keep going." -- Sam Levenson

"The will to win, the desire to succeed, the urge to reach your full potential... these are the keys that will unlock the door to personal excellence." -- Confucius

"It always seems impossible until it's done." -- Nelson Mandela

"Life is 10% what happens to you and 90% how you react to it." -- Charles R. Swindoll

"To succeed in your mission, you must have single-minded devotion to your goal." -- A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit." -- Aristotle

"Love all, trust a few, do wrong to none." -- William Shakespeare

"In 20 years, you probably won't even remember this. -- Anonymous

"Do not go where the path may lead, go instead where there is no path and leave a trail." -- Ralph Waldo Emerson

"Never cowardly or cruel. Never give up, never give in." -- Doctor Who

"Life may not be the party we hoped for, but while we're here, we should dance." -- Anonymous

"You can be the ripest, juiciest peach in the world and there's still going to be someone who hates peaches." -- Dita Von Teese

"Ask yourself if what you're doing today will get you closer to where you want to be tomorrow." -- Anonymous

"It's supposed to be hard. If it were easy, everyone would do it." -- Jimmy Dugan, A League of Their Own

"The way I see it, every life is a pile of good things and bad things. The good things don't always soften the bad things, but vice versa, the bad things don't always spoil the good things and make them unimportant." -- Doctor Who

"Do not set yourself on fire in order to keep others warm." -- Anonymous

"Do what you feel in your heart to be right, for you'll be criticized anyway." -- Eleanor Roosevelt.

"Plant your garden and decorate your own soul, instead of waiting for someone to bring you flowers." -- Jose Luis Borges

"There is no elevator to success — you have to take the stairs." -- Anonymous

"Nothing in life is to be feared; it is only to be understood. Now is the time to understand more so that we may fear less." -- Marie Curie

"Happiness is an inside job." -- Anonymous

"Believe you can and you're halfway there." -- Theodore Roosevelt

"I can be changed by what happens to me but I refuse to be reduced by it." -- Maya Angelou

"It's OK to not be OK, as long as you don't stay that way." -- Anonymous

"If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together." -- African proverb

"It's not about how hard you can hit; it's about how hard you can get hit and keep moving forward." -- Rocky Balboa, Rocky

"There isn't a way things should be. There's just what happens, and what we do." -- Terry Pratchett, A Hat Full of Sky

"No one can make you feel inferior without your consent." -- Eleanor Roosevelt

"Life's too mysterious to take too serious." -- Mary Engelbreit

"Keep your eyes on the stars and your feet on the ground." -- Theodore Roosevelt

"Be who you are and say what you feel, because those who mind don't matter and those who matter don't mind." -- Dr. Seuss

"Experience shows that success is due less to ability than to zeal." -- Charles Buxton

"I know the price of success: dedication, hard work, and an unremitting devotion to the things you want to see happen." -- Frank Lloyd Wright

"Opportunity is missed by most people because it is dressed in overalls and looks like work." -- Thomas Edison

"The purpose of education is to replace an empty mind with an open one." -- Malcolm S. Forbes

"Be thankful for what you have; you'll end up having more. If you concentrate on what you don't have, you will never, ever have enough." -- Oprah Winfrey

"Make the most of yourself by fanning the tiny, inner sparks of possibility into flames of achievement." -- Golda Meir

"Beauty is being the best possible version of yourself." -- Audrey Hepburn
