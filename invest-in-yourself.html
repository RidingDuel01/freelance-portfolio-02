Invest In Yourself, Today!

No matter what is being said, always, always, always try to be better than the person you were yesterday. Improve yourself everyday and always know that anything you set your mind to you can and will achieve them, as long as you give it everything you got. Don't ever believe that there is something you can't do. In order to know that you can't do something, you must first try it than give up, because if you keep on trying than there's a possibly that it's possible and we don't want that, right? lol. Never stop improving... Period!

Below are some tips that I believe will assist you on this trecherous journey to becoming a better version of yourself. Enjoy and absord the knowledge of others and make them your own... word of the wise, hahaha!

Talk Less, Listen More.

You understand things from a different point-of-view when you are the one listening instead of talking. Believe it or not, talking isn't the only way to have a conversation. Listen to the sounds around you and absorb them.

Learn something new everyday.

Learning something new everyday opens the mind in ways that you can't even imagine. Learning will help you look at things from a different perspective, instead of being closed minded, which won't get you far with anything.

Read, Read, Read and than Read some more.

Reading is key. Believe it or not, reading is not just beneficial to your mind. It benefits your body also. Just think about it, if you never learned that fire will burn you; you will potentially put you body in harms way. If you are under a lot of stress, that will increase cortisol, the primary stress hormone, which could put your health at risk. So feeding your mind will not only give you knowledge but can also reduce your stress. Read, Read, Read and let your mind explore.

Smile like the joker, HAHAHA Batman pun.

A happy, positive expression on your face will make life that much more awesomer. This is particularly true in challenging situations. A smiling, relaxed persona indicates an ability to cope well in stressful situations and confidence. Smiling will help you look at things in a positive light, instead of focusing on the negative, which won't get you anywhere in life. Focus on positivity in your life, and you can always start with a big smile. LOL!

Enjoy Every Moment

Most of the time, life doesn’t give us much time to stop and enjoy the view. It’s amazing that by simply changing your thinking in this way, you can get a richer experience of life, more success and more happiness. The reality is that you can only change the present moment. The past is gone and you can only affect the future by the actions you take in the present. You should therefore have all of your mental resources focused on delivering in the present moment. This will in turn make your life experience just that much better. Give it a try.
