Now let's answer your questions about our topics:

Now of course I'm not going to be making up topics out of thin air. You will know these topics indeed. But, this site plan on helping you view it from all angles for the best possible motivation and inspiration around. I don't just want to tell you the same information you see everywhere... you can get that anywhere. What I want is to have you to better understand these topics whether it be working for your lifestyle or even developing a sound mindset.I want to get you to understand that anything is possible, but, just because you copy someone elses formula doesn't mean you'll have the same outcome as that person. It might be because that formula was best suited for them, and knowing that you have to understand what you need to do in order to create a formula that best suits you.

Mindset

Thinking in a way where you see reality fr what it is and not for what you think it is. Setting up the mind to remove those shades and see the world for it true self.

Awareness

I basically mention and explain this in the mission on the homepage. But, this will talk about how you understand and why you should understand things that happen they way they do and to not be angry but accepting.

Focus

Within this topic, we will talk about how important keeping in mind your big picture is when it comes to accomplishing anything. Always keep in mind that big picture of success. Stay focused and achieve your goals.

Goals

Within this topics, we'll be talking about accomplishing goals. I know sometimes it can be very difficult at times to start and finish a goal you set out for yourself to complete. Well in order for something to be impossible, no one must do it, but, the second someone does do it, then it becomes possible. So instead of waiting for someone else to do it, make that someone be you.

Self-Development

Here we'll talk about when there is a challenge in front of you that you need to face in order to continue moving forward simultaneously crafting yourself into a better version of yourself.

Time

Time is something that is valuable to each and every one of us. That's the one thing you can never get back, no matter how hard you try. Within this topic it will talk about how you should focus on working hard now, so that you can have more time to enjoy more things you want to do in the future. That my friends is called investing in yourself.

Happiness

Here obviously we'll be talking about happiness. But we're not going to be talking about "Oh, I wish my life was full of happiness therefore, it well be"..ahh, no. We'll be talking about how to work hard to obtain happiness.

Freedom

Within ths topic, we'll be talking about how to truly feel free. I don't think there is more I need to say.

All of the topics I mentioned above all intertwine with each other in some way. They all rely on one another in order to reap the full benefits.
