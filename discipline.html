Quotes of Discipline

"If we don't discipline ourselves, the world will do it for us." -- William Feather

"Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishment." -- Jim Rohn

"Discipline is remembering what you want." -- David Campbell

Did you enjoy the quotes I put above?... Yeah you did! Wanted you guys to read and understand what the message is trying to say. That anything you set your mind to you can and will achieve them, as long as you give it everything you got. Discipline plays an important role in achieving ones goal. Like that little rhyme there... yeah, all me!

These quotes really speak volumes about how important discipline is in ones quest to awesomeness and why each and every one of you us (I'm on this quest with you guys too) should practice and master the Art of Discipline.

Discipline makes easy becomes nothing, medium becomes easy, hard becomes medium, and expert becomes hard. With discipline you have less hard times because most of those hard times you confused them for easy.

In order to know that you can't do something, you must first try it than give up, because if you keep on trying than there's a possibly that it's possible and we don't want that, right? lol. Have the discipline to see things through to the end.
